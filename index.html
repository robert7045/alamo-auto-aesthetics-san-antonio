Frequently Asked Questions About Window Tinting

How long does window tinting last?

Our high-quality window tints are designed to last for many years, with proper maintenance and care. Most installations come with a lifetime warranty, guaranteeing their durability and resilience against fading and peeling.

Is window tinting legal in San Antonio?

Yes, window tinting is legal in San Antonio, provided it complies with Texas state regulations regarding visible light transmission. Our team ensures all installations meet legal standards, prioritizing your safety and compliance.

How to maintain and care for tinted windows?

To maintain the integrity of your tinted windows, avoid using abrasive cleaners or tools that may scratch the film. Regularly clean with a mild, non-ammonia solution and a soft cloth to preserve the tint's clarity and effectiveness.
